How to Play

Navigate to the Download page (found on the bar above). Download by clicking "Game (.zip)". Extract the downloaded zip file. Run "Turn-Based-RPG.exe" to open the game. The game is now running.
Navigate menus and move with WASD, press E to interact, and P to open the menu.
